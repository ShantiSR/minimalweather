The app will update its icon, temperature and colors depending on the current conditions so you can check it without entering the app.

Minimal Weather

Install Minimal Weather

Not only the temperature changes

Besides the change of the icon and the temperature the app and the icon will change colors. Colors let you realize quicker what is happening outside.

You have cold, temperate and hot temperatures. Each one of those for the day and night.

It's not about the numbers, it's about the feeling

The temperature you will see is not the actual temperature but the apparent temperature.

You'll get feedback on the temperature you'll feel outside. Certain conditions like humidity and wind speed can change the way you feel outside so the app takes care of that for you.

Be prepared for change

Sometimes it's a great day outside and suddenly your picnic gets screwed by the rain. Now you can see if there's a chance of rain important enough to get your umbrella.

The app will show you a little umbrella if there's a chance of raining in the next 8 hours so you can prepare.
